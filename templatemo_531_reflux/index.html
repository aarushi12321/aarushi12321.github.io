Interships and Projects

Here are a list of other internships and projects that I have done over the course of my B.Tech.

Research Intern, SAI Labs

Worked under the supervision of Prof. B.B. Gupta and Dragan Perakovic at SAI Labs. Implemented federated machine learning algorithm (CNN + ANN) to train a model for traffic prediction, time-duration prediction and origin-destination prediction. Contributed in developing a federated learning framework that can be integrated with autoencoder at client side. Compared head and body based fake-news detection models with encryption and distributed learning techniques.

Project Intern, Prutor.ai

Worked under the supervision of Mr. Rahul Garg to develop a Susceptible-Infected-Removed (SIR) model to predict the peak and flattening of curve for second wave of COVID. Developed baseline statistics and machine learning models and compared their accuracy to make predictions based on previous trends. Developed inferences and analyzed the COVID second wave data, factors affecting the course of the pandemic.

Federated Learning framework for smart transportation systems

The project focuses on building a federated learning framework for smart transportation system focusing on features such as origin-destination prediction, trip duration prediction and traffic flow prediction. It makes use of a basic dataset - New York City Taxi Trip Duration dataset - to generate new data to use it for federated learning framework.

Privacy techniques and best practices for fake news detection

The project compares Headline and Body attributes for fake news detection for different models. Different framework of models i.e. centralized, federated and federated plus differential privacy framework are experimented with and compared to find best frameworks. Tree based algorithms, RNN and BERT are used with the aforementioned frameworks to compare results and performances in differnt scenarios.
